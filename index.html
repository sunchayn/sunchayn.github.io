About

A small biography about myself that shows how I dived into the field of IT and what I've done.

I'm 22 old full-stack web developer from Tunisia. My passion with code started years ago, almost 8 years now.

I carry both technical and academic knowledge. Currently, I'm studying for my media engineering master's degree and I hold a bachelor degree in computer science. Despite my formal education, I made my self busy learning and working with versatile technologies. Throughout the years, I acquired the ability to solve problems and to adapt to changes. Performance and usability are a priority for me, I always seek to write a suitable code for both humans and computers. As an innovator individual, I'm able to dive into new challenges and to explore new ideas.

I created a variety of open source projects alongside private projects in my freelancing career. I'm the kind of person who likes to share knowledge and teach people what he knows which made me, with all humility, a reliable mentor for my university's colleagues. When we talk about teams I'm ready to be that strong link in the chain and be a part of technical decisions.

When it comes to my philosophy, I believe in simplicity. I prefer going straight to the point. Generally, I aim to avoid the solutions or libraries that are stuffed with unused code. These unexplored parts will make the developer out of control. For each problem, there's a specific solution and I'm continuously thrilled to find out that particular solution.

" You have succeeded in life when all you really want is only what you really need " - Vernon Howard

Lately, I've started thinking about blogging seriously. Well, I had been an active member in forums where I shared and discussed technical subjects. Now, I have the motivation to write and share my thoughts with the world.
